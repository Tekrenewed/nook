# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Foodies who appreciate high-quality ingredients, premium smash burgers, and a great dining experience.

## Product Purpose
A promotional landing page and menu to showcase Nook Burgers, drive appetite, and convey the premium quality of the food.

## Brand Personality
Bold, Editorial, Premium.

## Anti-references
Generic fast-food templates, cheap bright red/yellow aesthetics, standard AI-generated layouts.

## Design Principles
- Show, don't tell (let the photography do the work).
- Bold confidence (confident spacing, stark contrast).
- High-end execution (impeccable typography and rhythm).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA contrast minimums. Reduced motion support.
