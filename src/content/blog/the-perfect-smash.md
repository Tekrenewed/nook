# The Anatomy of the Perfect Smash Burger

At Nook Burgers, we don't just cook beef—we transform it through extreme heat and pressure. The secret behind our signature crust is a scientific process called the **Maillard Reaction**.

## The Science of Smashed Beef
When our premium 100% Halal beef hits the searing hot flat-top grill, we immediately smash it flat. This creates maximum contact area with the heat.

- **Extreme Heat:** Searing at high temperatures ensures a rapid chemical reaction.
- **Maximum Pressure:** Smashing locks the juices inside the patty while crisping the outside.
- **Minimal Interference:** We use a "less is more" philosophy. We don't hide behind dozens of sauces; we let the quality of the meat speak for itself.

### Our Ingredients
Every element of a Nook Burger is designed with intention.

1. **The Beef**: Dry-aged, perfectly balanced lean-to-fat ratio.
2. **The Bun**: A classic, soft potato bun that holds its structure without overpowering the meat.
3. **The Cheese**: Perfectly melted to bind the patty and bun together.
